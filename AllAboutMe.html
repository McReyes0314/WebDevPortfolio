Process

In this activity, we were assigned to include the following basic details about ourselves in the provided template:

  • A recent picture of ourselves.
  • Our full name.
  • A description of ourselves using three words that highlight our qualities, hobbies, or anything that represents us.
  • A short message sharing our impression of our instructor.
  • Lastly, the three topics we want to learn throughout this year.

Learnings

Through this activity, I learned the following:

  • By listing the three things I want to learn this year, I gained clarity on the areas I want to focus on.
  • I was able to set my expectations for both the subject and the instructor
  • Reflecting on my qualities helped me better understand and define who I am.
